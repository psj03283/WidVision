Raising Awareness through Public Relations

1. WORK

The Public Relations Office of WidVision plays a crucial role in communicating and promoting the organization's objectives and achievements to the public.

2. MEDIA

The department is responsible for executing strategic media campaigns, including publishing informative articles and news stories in various print and digital platforms.

3. AWARENESS

The aim is to create a positive image of the organization and to increase public understanding of WidVision's efforts in improving eye health and providing better treatment options for individuals with visual impairments.

4. PROMOTION

Additionally, the department regularly releases updates on the company's business plan and events to keep the public informed about WidVision's progress and future endeavors.

Cooperation with Seoul Scholars times journal

The WidVision Public Relations Office has recently made a significant contribution to the dissemination of information about eye health through the publication of articles in the highly regarded Scholars Times Journal. 

Scholars Times Publication

The articles are the result of a comprehensive and collaborative research effort between the Public Relations Office and the team at the SSI newspaper. This collaboration between the communication and journalism professionals has produced a series of articles that are not only insightful but also grounded in rigorous research and fact-checking.

WidVision, a leading provider of eye health solutions, has demonstrated its dedication to sharing its extensive knowledge and expertise with a wider audience through its partnership with highly regarded publications such as the Scholars Times. 

This collaboration reflects WidVision's commitment to contributing to the growth and development of the field of eye health, and to increasing public awareness of the importance of good eye health.
