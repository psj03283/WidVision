About WidVision Program

WidVision is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with visual impairments. Our organization consists of three primary departments: the Central Executive Office, the Information Technology Office, and the Public Relations Office. 

Each department works cohesively towards the shared goal of creating a supportive and inclusive environment for the blind community, guided by our unifying motto, 'Together with Blind Community'.

Please note that WidVision is currently in the process of being officially registered as a non-profit organization. The registration process involves a comprehensive verification period of one year, after which the organization will be granted authorization to operate. 

WidVision is an established organization that will continue its operations beyond the graduation of its current Chief Officer, Sungju Park. Upon the Chief Officer's graduation, WidVision will be detached from the student clubs at SSI.

Vision for 
a Better World

The aging population is causing a rise in major eye diseases and vision loss globally, resulting in a public health issue. The World Health Organization's World Report on Vision states that 2.2 billion people worldwide have vision impairment, with 1 billion being preventable or yet to be addressed. This highlights the need for action to address the issue and improve vision globally.
